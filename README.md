Mod2
A math puzzle browser game. Combine numbers with operations to score points.
How to Play
Tap a number, then tap an adjacent operation tile (+, −, ×, ÷) to lock it in
Tap another adjacent number to complete the math
Even results → +1 point
Odd results → −1 point
Result of 0 → creates a blank space you can move through
Results split into individual digit tiles (e.g., 5 × 8 = 40 → a "4" tile + a blank)
Reach the goal score to advance to the next level!
Strategy Tips
Multiplication is your friend for producing even numbers
Engineer zeros with subtraction (same number minus itself) to open movement lanes
Use blank spaces to reposition tiles into better setups
Think ahead — every operation changes the board
