How to Play

Tap a number next to an operation, then tap the operation, then tap a second adjacent number to complete it. Numbers must be directly above, below, or beside — no diagonals.

The result determines your score:

Even = +1 Odd = −1 Zero = blank

A zero result leaves blank tiles on the board. Select a number and tap an adjacent blank to slide it — use this to reposition numbers strategically.

Reach the goal score to level up. The board grows and goals get higher. Levels go on forever — how far can you climb?
